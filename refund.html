Introduction

At FUN2SH Enterprise, we are committed to delivering premium consultancy and business growth services to our clients. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel or request a refund for services. This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains the terms under which you may request a refund or cancel services booked through FUN2SH Enterprise.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your service request at any time prior to the commencement of the service. Please notify us of your cancellation by contacting us via email at info@fun2shenterprise.com or by calling our customer support line directly.

Cancellations for scheduled services must be made at least 48 hours in advance to avoid any charges. If you cancel within 48 to 24 hours of the scheduled service time, a cancellation fee equivalent to 30% of the service cost will be charged. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service time will incur a 50% cancellation fee.

Refund Policy

FUN2SH Enterprise offers refunds under the following conditions:

  • If a service is canceled more than 48 hours before the scheduled time, a full refund will be issued.
  • If the service is canceled within 48 to 24 hours of the scheduled time, you will receive a refund of 70% of the total service fee.
  • If you cancel less than 24 hours before the service or after the service has already been delivered, no refund will be granted.
  • In case of a service not meeting the agreed-upon terms, you can request a refund by contacting us with evidence of unmet service expectations. We will assess the situation and process any appropriate refund within 14 business days.

Non-Refundable Services

Refunds are not applicable for the following services:

  • Consultation sessions that have already taken place.
  • Reports, market research, or financial analysis that has been delivered.
  • Legal or tax advisory services once completed.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to info@fun2shenterprise.com with the following details:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Order details (invoice or transaction number)
  • The reason for the refund request

We will review your request and provide feedback within 7 business days. Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days of the approval notice.

Changes to the Policy

FUN2SH Enterprise reserves the right to update or change this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our services following these updates will signify your acceptance of the revised terms.
